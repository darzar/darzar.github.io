Well Being Support






Diet and Lifestyle go hand in hand to optimize overall well being.

Learn more about how to identify stress, identify stress triggers and understand the impacts stress has on overall health and functioning.

Diana will support you in learning more about easy, active stress reduction and stress management strategies you can incorporate into your daily routine, regardless of how busy your life is.




RETREATS
Twice a year Diana works together with Selina Gullery, an expert yoga instructor and meditation guide. Together they host a fabulous retreat in a wonderful chalet in the beautiful mountains of Verbier.

Their daily retreat schedule includes:

• Guided meditation and mindfulness sessions
• Clean, nutrient dense meals
• Two daily yoga sessions (adapted for all levels to ensure each individual makes progress at their own level)
• Guided mountain walks and hikes for all fitness levels
• Food demonstrations
• Food education
• Individualised nutritional therapy sessions (on request)

Diana looks forwards to collaborating with more professionals in the greater Geneva area in order to offer more group mindfulness, meditation, breathing and yoga sessions.
