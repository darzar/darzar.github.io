Nutritional Support

Nutritional Therapy is a personalised approach to improving overall health through diet and lifestyle. Diana is trained to follow a Functional Medicine approach to understanding the root cause of one’s concerns and ailments. She works on a 1-1 basis in order to really personalise her recommendations to best suit each individual clients needs. Nutritional therapy focuses on investigating the causes of the signs and symptoms that make you feel: • Tired, low energy, experience energy slumps • Indigestion, bloated, gassy, constipated, diarrhoea, cramps, nausea • unable to sleep or wake up during the night • jittery, irritable, moody • skin issues, bleeding gums, • low mood, anxiety, high stress, wired, brain fog • food allergies and intolerances • hormonal imbalances, frequent illness • difficulty losing or gaining weight • sugar cravings • and much more If you feel generally unwell and find yourself suffering from the same discomfort for years but have never been able to figure out why Nutritional Therapy can help support you in learning more about how what you are eating is affecting your body, how you feel, impact disease potential and your overall health. Learn more about how to heal and maximize optimal health through nutrition. Diana will work with you one on one in a supportive and confidential setting. Through collating a detailed health history, identifying your top concerns, conducting a full analysis of signs and symptoms and look at your current diet and lifestyle to determine the best personalized recommendations for you to feel better, stronger and more energetic. What can you expect with Nutritional therapy?? • You will receive a very detailed, comprehensive pre consultation form to fill out prior to your first meeting. This will allow Diana to access the background information she needs to better understand your health and lifestyle concerns, your current dietary and lifestyle habits, in addition to knowing what signs and symptoms you are currently experiencing. • During your first meeting (which lasts 1.5-2 hours), Diana will collect more personal health related information through a detailed series of questions in order to fully understand you as a client. • Diana will then discuss the connections, likely triggers and possible causes of what you are experiencing. • You will receive a first series of personalised recommendations based on your identified needs, concerns, signs and symptoms. You will also receive further information related to the connecting triggers and likely causes supporting these recommendations • Diana will support you with dietary recommendations, recipes, kitchen demonstration sessions, cooking lessons, food education and more to make implementing change as easy as possible. • Most clients require 2-3 follow up sessions to review how initial recommendations were implemented and review improvements accordingly. Diana is a member of NTOI, BANT, IINH, and various specialized laboratories. This enables her to keep informed of latest research studies, attend conferences and access specialised professionals for referrals, if needed. Access to laboratories enables Diana to recommend specific testing to determine suspected findings and tailor recommendations accordingly.
