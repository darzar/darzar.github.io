Nutritional Support

As a Nutritional Therapist, Diana has a particular interest in identifying underlying causes that link to diffculties in learning, effect behavior, and mood. She works with parents to learn more about the childs:
• health history [personal and family]
• medication
• history of antibiotics
• genetic factors
• current diet
• sleep/stress/exercise
and much more, to determine what might be triggering the signs and symptoms the child is displaying and how to identify the likely causes. Through a personalized and investigative approach, she will put together a personal protocal to support the improving of overall health, and well-being through dietary and lifestyle changes.



Diana also works with adults and follows a similar investigative approach to determine the individual signs, symptoms, and top concerns that make you feel:
• Tired, low energy, experience energy slumps
• Indigestion, bloated, gassy, constipated, diarrhoea, cramps, nausea
• unable to sleep or wake up during the night
• jittery, irritable, moody
• skin issues, bleeding gums
• low mood, anxiety, high stress, wired, brain fog
• food allergies and intolerances
• hormonal imbalances, frequent illness
• difficulty losing or gaining weight
• sugar cravings
• and much more

Diana is trained to follow a Functional Medicine approach to understanding the root cause of one’s concerns and issues. She works on a 1-1 basis in order to really personalise her recommendations to best suit each individual client's needs.

If you feel generally unwell and find yourself suffering from the same discomfort for years but have never been able to figure out why, Nutritional Therapy can help support you in learning more about how what you are eating is affecting your body, how you feel, impact disease potential and your overall health. Learn more about how to heal and maximize optimal health through nutrition.

Diana will work with you one on one in a supportive and confidential setting. Through collating a detailed health history, identifying your top concerns, conducting a full analysis of signs and symptoms and look at your current diet and lifestyle to determine the best personalized recommendations for you to feel better, stronger and more energetic.

What can you expect with Nutritional Therapy??
Whether the focus is on you or your child:

• You will receive a very detailed, comprehensive pre consultation form to fill out prior to your first meeting. This will allow Diana to access the background information she needs to better understand your health and lifestyle concerns, your current dietary and lifestyle habits, in addition to knowing what signs and symptoms you are currently experiencing.
• During your first meeting (which lasts 1.5-2 hours), Diana will collect more personal health related information through a detailed series of questions in order to fully understand you and your needs as a client.
• Diana will then discuss the connections, likely triggers and possible causes of what you are experiencing.
• You will receive a first series of personalised recommendations based on your identified needs, concerns, signs and symptoms. You will also receive further information related to the connecting triggers and likely causes supporting these recommendations
• Diana will support you with dietary recommendations, recipes, kitchen demonstration sessions, cooking lessons, food education and more to make implementing change as easy as possible.
• Most clients require 2-3 follow up sessions to review how initial recommendations were implemented and review improvements accordingly. Diana is a member of NTOI, BANT, IINH, and various specialized laboratories. This enables her to keep informed of latest research studies, attend conferences and access specialised professionals for referrals, if needed.

• FOOD MATTERS EDUCATION
While we all like to think we are eating healthy and making the best decisions for ourselves and for our growing children we sometimes question if that is really the case. Join ‘food matters’ to learn more about why the decisions you make everyday to nourish your bodies, your brains and your well-being are so important. A four week course that answers the questions you have and provides thorough information on how to make good choices.
