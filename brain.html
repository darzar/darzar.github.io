Educational Support

• Provides biannual academic assessments to determine areas of weakness and to asses progress accordingly.
• Develops Individual Education Plans to outline individual student objectives and provides educational therapy to support meeting these objectives
• Provides 1-1 sessions to meet identified needs 1-2 times per week
• Supports students using a ‘learn to learn’ approach using multisensory methods to maximize pathways to the brain.
• Individualizes support to address and support student’s weaknesses while continuing to build on their strengths
• Provides revisions materials to support ‘little but often’ revision opportunities to consolidate learning.
• Facilitates parent meetings to discuss student progress, family concerns, issues/concerns related to school.


With her unique and diverse qualifications, background and experience, Diana has chosen to combine her interests and support students with Specific Learning Difficulties in a more holistic manner.

She will identify, through academic assessments, the ‘gaps’ in learning of each individual and develop an Individual Education Plan to work towards achieving identified learning objectives.

In addition, she will make Nutritional Therapy Support available to all clients in order to develop a better understanding of how nutrition impacts the functioning of the brain.

With over 20 years combined teaching and practice experience. Diana is passionate about supporting students with Specific Learning Difficulties and has developed a particular interest in combining Educational therapy with Nutritional therapy to focus on maximizing brain development and functioning through diet and lifestyle. As a qualified chef she has the expertise and passion to support families with introducing nutrient rich foods into their diets while easily removing nutrient void foods, which have a toxic impact on the brain, on learning and on behaviour.
